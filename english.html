Thronebound: cards and battles

The game goal is to defeat the enemy duo, using the characters skills and distribute cards.

Two duos are necessary to play.

O jogo contém:

  • 1 board;
  • 4 characters totems;
  • 24 cards: 4 characters cards, 4 fixed cards (weapons), one to each character and 16 support cards;
  • 240 damage markers: 40 with value '10', 40 with value '-10', 40 with value '+10', 40 with value '5', 40 with value '-5' and 40 with value '+5'.
  • 4 dices with 20 faces (d20).

Meet the characters

Thoma, the bard

Thoma is a poet and bard that live of music, traveling and singing through the taverns on his way. He likes to create great and a little distorted from reality, but even so, he is still a great artist.

Ayla, the viking

Ayla has been a warrior since she was a little girl, her father taught her all the techniques for a good fight, from how to use a sword and an ax, to a shield. Even though she trained for the battlefront, she tries to be as sneaky as possible.

Alyss, the crossbowgirl

Alyss is a hunter who likes to live in the silence of the forest. With your crossbow and splendid aim your hunt always brings success. She started her career as a hunter, but after so much training, she decided to become a renowned bounty hunter.

Carmesin, the witcher

Carmesim is an extremely skilled mysterious sorceress, her potions and spells make everyone have nightmares. Her origin is uncertain, but as she is the village's healer, no one dares to question her knowledge.

ATQ/ DEF additional

  • ATQ: character's original attack value;
  • DEF: value of the character's original protection (which can be increased with weapons, cards or support characters);
  • DPS: responsible for inflicting greater damage;
  • Support: helps the DPS, being able to increase its damage or protection, for example;
  • HP: character’s life total;
  • ATQ bonus: bonus value added to the attack that is raffled by the dice.

Organizing the game

  • Initially duos (4 people, 2 against 2) or a single duo (2 people, 1 against 1) are defined. Then, the duo must choose a duo of characters, namely: Thoma + Ayla or Alyss + Crimson (each member chooses their specific character);
  • The player receives the character's totem, along with his card, the fixed card (weapon) indicated on the character's card and a 20-sided die. To start, each duo rolls a dice and the one with the highest number starts the game;
  • After defining the order and receiving the objects mentioned above, a support card is shuffled for each player (2 support cards per duo). The rest must be shuffled and placed in a stack and left beside the board.

Initial rules

  • During the game, the player must "narrate" his movements, saying who will attack and how he will attack (which attack or skill he will use);
  • After being used, the cards must be placed in a discard stack, for when the stack purchase run out, it must be shuffled and placed in the purchase stack again;
  • The character card “Carmesim, the witcher” has the ability to summon the support card “Sombra”. By doing this, the support card must be given to the player with the character card “Carmesim, the witcher”, even being with another player or in the discard stack;
  • Each card has instructions of use. Pay attention to the description! It contains its effect and the number of turns it can last;
  • The turn starts with the winning duo of the dice ruffle. It ends when it comes back to that same duo;
  • At every turn, both duos play once. In the duo’s turn, each player can only play once, according to your will, not exceeding the rules;
  • The duos/players cannot take a support card from the purchase stack in the first turn. In the next turns, the duo must take a support card when it’s their turn to play;
  • The player can only do one move per turn, except the one who’s playing with more than one character. This one must play for two in the same shift;
  • The player's turn ends when they attack.

Starting the game

  • At the beginning of each turn (except the first of each one) the duo must buy 1 support card from the purchase stack;
  • Support cards must be used after purchase a card from the purchase stack and before starting your attack;
  • Support cards must be used after purchase a card from the purchase stack and before starting your attack;
  • Attacks are made using the ATQ bonuses or character's skills;
  • To attack, the player chooses the type of attack: fixed Attack + ATQ bonus + Fixed card (weapon) or fixed attack + one character skill + fixed card (weapon);
  • After defining which attack to do, the player must roll the dice according to the percentages (the percentage of each character is described on it’s card):
    • 25%: numbers from 1 to 5;
    • 50%: numbers from 6 to 10;
    • 75%: numbers from 11 to 15;
    • If the dice number is different from those mentioned above, the player doesn’t receive any bonuses and any abilities of the character
  • To mark the damage suffered or defense/attack bonuses provided by a card, the player must use the counters and place them on his character's card;
  • The game ends when both players in a duo have 0 life or surrender.
